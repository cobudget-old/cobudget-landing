The Story of Cobudget

Cobudget is a cooperative social enterprise incubated within Enspiral, the social enterprise network responsible for Loomio. We wondered if we could simplify and democratize our collective budget (13 social enterprises) while making it more fun and engaging. The result is Cobudget, a collaborative funding tool that allows groups of any size to transparently manage shared budgets.
